# The Indian American <span class="accent-name">Attitudes Survey</span>

With [Devesh Kapur](https://sais.jhu.edu/users/dkapur1) and [Milan Vaishnav](https://carnegieendowment.org/people/milan-vaishnav), I co-direct the Indian American Attitudes Survey (IAAS) 
— the largest ongoing study of the political and social attitudes of Indian
Americans, published with the Carnegie Endowment for International Peace.
